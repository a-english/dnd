Gloves of Thievery

These gloves are light to the touch and fit closely with the skin. These gloves were crafted to honor Puck, and then ironically stolen.

Wearing these gloves somehow makes you feel like you can do anything. When wearing these gloves, you gain a +5 to Dexterity checks for Sleight of Hand and picking locks. These gloves are also invisible when worn.With every mischievous deed committed, the wearer seems to hear pan flutes in the distance.

Major uncommon item Attunement: yes
