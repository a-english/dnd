Dagger of Shooting Stars

This finely-wrought dagger appeared at a mysterious shrine after wishing upon a star.

Like all daggers it is a finesse weapon that can be thrown or used to stab for 1d4 damage, plus either your Strength or Dexterity.

Once per day, it can be used to cast Magic Missile as a first-level spell. You create three glowing darts of magical force. Each dart hits a creature of your choice that you can see within 120ft. A dart deals 1d4+1 force damage to its target. The darts all strike simultaneously, and you can direct them to hit one creature or several.
