Staff of Charming

This staff was recovered from the satyr prince's palace deep within the Feywild. It can only be attuned to by a bard, cleric, druid, sorceror, warlock, or wizard.

While holding this staff, you can use an action to expend some of it 10 charges to cast a spell using your spell save DC.

Spell NameCost
Charm Person1
Command1
Comprehend Languages1

The staff can also be used as a magic quarterstaff. Like all quarterstaffs, it is a versatile weapon that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage when wielded with one hand, or 1d8 when wielded with two.

If you are holding the staff and fail a saving throw against an enchantment spell that targets only you, you can turn your failed save into a successful one. You can't use this property of the staff again until the next dawn. If you succeed on a save against an enchantment wpell that targets only you, with or without the staff's intervention, you can use your reaction to expend 1 charge from the staff and turn the spell back on its caster as if you had cast the spell.

The staff regains 1d8+2 expended charges daily at dawn.
